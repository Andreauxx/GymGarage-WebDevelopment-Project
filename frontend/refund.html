Refund Policy

At GymGarage, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not, we offer the following refund policy.

Eligibility for Refund

Products are eligible for a refund within 30 days of purchase, provided they are in their original packaging, unused, and accompanied by a valid receipt or proof of purchase.

Non-Refundable Items
  • Customized or personalized items
  • Clearance items
  • Digital products (e.g., e-books, software)
Refund Process

To initiate a refund, please contact us at support@gymgarage.com with your order number and reason for the return. Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the status of your refund.

Refund Payment

Approved refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 7-10 business days. Please note that processing times may vary depending on your financial institution.
